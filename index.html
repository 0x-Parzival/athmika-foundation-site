A Leader's Journey of Service and Excellence

Amarjeet Kumar represents the epitome of visionary leadership combined with compassionate service. As the founder of Aathmika Foundation, established in 2016, he has dedicated his life to creating meaningful change in society.

With an unwavering commitment to social welfare, Amarjeet has emerged as a beacon of hope for countless individuals across India. His foundation focuses on comprehensive support for disability care, elderly assistance, education advancement, and health improvement initiatives.

Under his leadership, Aathmika Foundation has touched the lives of over 50,000 children and continues to expand its reach across 72 countries, embodying the true spirit of global humanitarian service.
